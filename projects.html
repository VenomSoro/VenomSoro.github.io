Medical Kits for Daggerfall Unity

Adds a new item to Daggerfall Unity. The item has custom funcionality that relies on the players current medical skills and some related attributes to determine how much health is restored and how long it takes to heal. Generally, those with a high medical skill will heal more and spend less time using the kit. Along with this, a higher medical skill results in more efficient usage of the kits and allows for additional functionality such as treating diseases and poisons.

You can read about the mod more on Nexus and look at the code here.

Random Magical Trinkets for Daggerfall Unity

Adds a luck-based loot system to Daggerfall Unity. Based upon the player's luck, a random trinket (weapon, armor, clothing, or jewelry) has a chance to generate on slain enemies and in dungeon loot piles. Depending on how lucky they are, the item can be of a higher quality, resulting in the item having more effects and said effects being stronger. The effects are randomly chosen on generation, meaning that depending on pure chance, a player could potentially receive a powerful trinket with a combination of useful effects.

You can read about the mod more on Nexus and look at the code here.
